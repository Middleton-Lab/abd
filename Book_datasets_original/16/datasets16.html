Here are the data sets used in The Analysis of Biological Data in chapter 16, in .csv (comma-separated) format. Most data sets used in the book are grabbed from graphs and tables in papers, and the values may not be exact.

----Wiseman, R. and P. Lamont. 1996. Nature 383: 212-213.
