Here are the data sets used in The Analysis of Biological Data in chapter 15, in .csv (comma-separated) format. Most data sets used in the book are grabbed from graphs and tables in papers, and the values may not be exact.

Examples:

Example 15.1: Knees who say night
----inferred from Wright Jr., K. P. and C. A. Czeisler 2002. Science 297: 571.

Example 15.6: Walking sticks
----Nosil, P. and B. J. Crespi. 2006. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences 103:9090-9095.
